11/2-11/4: BGIN Block #4 meeting (Virtual Nairobi/Lome/Accra)

Date / Time

  • November 2 - July 4, 2021 (Tuesday - Thursday)
  • 12:00 pm - 15:00 UTC

Registration

Block #4 Registration Form

One conference zoom link for all three days will be emailed to participants beforehand.

Who should attend?

  • Developers
  • Regulators
  • Business entities
  • Consumers
  • Civil society
  • Academia
  • And anyone interested in blockchain ecosystems!
