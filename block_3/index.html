6/29-7/1: BGIN Block #3 meeting (Virtual DC/NYC)

by Geoff-Livingston

Date / Time

  • June 29 - July 1, 2021 (Tuesday - Thursday)
  • 15:00 - 18:00 UTC https://everytimezone.com/s/ada4bcbb

Registration

Registration here: https://forms.gle/LWBLB5niRFz8b6Fr9

One conference zoom link for all three days will be emailed to participants beforehand.

Who should attend?

  • Developers
  • Regulators
  • Business entities
  • Consumers
  • Civil society
  • Academia
  • And anyone interested in blockchain ecosystems!

Agenda (in progress)

Day 1: Tuesday, June 29 * 15:00-18:00 UTC

15:00-16:00 Opening Plenary [BGIN Draft Code of Conduct] [BGIN Draft Note Well]
  • Opening Plenary
  • Welcome & Introductions
    • Opening Remarks by Mai Santamaria
    • Welcome from local hosts Reena Aggarwal and Aaron J. Wright
    • Introducing BGIN by Shin’ichiro Matsuo
  • Reports and introduction of Day 2 sessions from BGIN Working Groups
    • Internal Governance Working Group: Shigeya Suzuki and Aaron J. Wright
    • IAM, Privacy & Key Management Working Group: Nat Sakimura
  • Report from BGIN Steering Committee: Shin’ichiro Matsuo
  • Report from Bylaw Task Force: Dhan Iyer and Bryan Cute
16:00-17:20 Invited talks on Future of Blockchain Development
  • Regulator: Jonathan Fishman, Co-chair of FATF VACG (Virtual Assets Contact Group)
  • Business: Sarah Olsen, Head of Business Development for Onyx, JP Morgan
  • Engineer: Jay Berg, DexChain
  • Legislator: Darren Soto (D-FL)
17:20-18:00 Open discussion (Invited speakers as panelists + moderator + discussion with all attendees)
  • Moderator and panelists (to be confirmed)
  • Governance of permissionless blockchain: AML/KYC/Privacy and more

Day 2: Wednesday, June 30 * 15:00-18:00 UTC + 1 extra hour

15:00-16:30 Internal Governance Working Group (Chairs: Shigeya Suzuki and Aaron Wright)
  • Report on IGWG activities
  • Open discussion about on-going and new topics with all participants
16:30-18:00 IAM, Key Management and Privacy Working Group (Chair: Nat Sakimura)
  • Introduction of IKP WG/SGIP
  • Briefings on three documents in progress

    • Present and Future of a Decentralized Financial System and the associated Regulatory Considerations
    • https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Tko_ERfXBpb8XE4BjvBHVWduFKcq_pIwIriSVZwu_Ic/edit?usp=sharing
  • Inputs from participants
  • Invited talks
    • Nomura Research Institute representative (SSI/DID)
    • Claudio Levrini, CEO & Co-founder, Seven Labs (BFT)
  • New work item proposals
  • Open discussion
  • Any other business
18:00-19:00 Unconference sessions & social hour + brainstorming in multiple breakout rooms - proposals for topics are welcomed (please send email to organizers bgin-block3-admin @ bgin.team )
  • Under Chatham House Rule https://chathamhouse.org/about-us/chatham-house-rule
  • Topic candidates to be determined
